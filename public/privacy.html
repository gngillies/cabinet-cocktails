Cabinet Cocktails

Privacy Policy

Overview

Cabinet Cocktails is a cocktail discovery app. You photograph your liquor cabinet, and the app identifies what cocktails you can make. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use the app.

Photos

When you take or upload a photo, it is sent securely to our server solely to be analysed by the Anthropic Claude AI. The photo is used only to identify bottles and generate cocktail suggestions. It is not stored, saved, or retained in any form after the analysis is complete. We do not have access to your photo library.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

— Your name, email address, or any account information
— Your location
— Your photos or camera roll
— Your device identifiers
— Usage analytics or behavioural data
— Cookies or tracking data

Third-Party Services

The app uses the Anthropic Claude API to analyse cabinet photos. Your photo is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for this purpose and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services receive your data.

Children

Cabinet Cocktails is intended for adults of legal drinking age. It is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. We will note the date of the most recent update below.
